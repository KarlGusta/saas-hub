How to Convert Bank Statements to CSV or JSON

A simple guide to turning raw bank data into usable formats.

Why Convert Bank Statements?

Bank statements are usually provided as PDFs, which are difficult to analyze or integrate into systems. Converting them into CSV or JSON allows easier processing, reporting, and automation.

Method 1: Manual Conversion

You can manually copy data from PDFs into spreadsheets. However, this is time-consuming and prone to errors, especially for large datasets.

Method 2: Use Automated Tools

Automated tools extract structured data directly from PDFs, saving time and improving accuracy.
